\begin{abstract}
  We use matrics to solve systems of linear equations
\end{abstract}
\maketitle



Any time we have a system of equations we represent it with a matrix:
\[
\begin{array}{ccccccc}
      3x & -&y&+&z&= &0 \\
     2x& +&y&+&2z&=&2\\
     x& +&4y&-&2z&=&11
\end{array}
\qquad\Rightarrow\qquad
\left(\begin{array}{ccc|c}
  3 & -1 & 1 & 0 \\
  2 &  1 & 2 & 2 \\
  1 &  4 & 2 & 11 
\end{array}\right)
\]
