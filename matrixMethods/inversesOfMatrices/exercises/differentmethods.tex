\begin{exercise}
For the matrix
\[A= \left(\begin{array}{cc}
-2 & 4 \\
0 & 5
\end{array}\right)
\]
a. Find $A^{-1}$ by applying the formula for the inverse of a $2\times 2$ matrix.\\
b. Find $A^{-1}$ by setting up $(A \hspace{.08cm}|\hspace{.08cm} I)$. \\
c. Find $A^{-1}$ by using $AA^{-1}= A^{-1}A=I$. 
\end{exercise}
